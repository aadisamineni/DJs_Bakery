Our Story

DJ’s Bakery is a family-run neighborhood bakery that’s been part of Ann Arbor mornings for years. What began as a small family passion has grown into a welcoming place where locals stop by for fresh donuts, strong Vietnamese coffee, and familiar faces behind the counter.

Everything we serve is made fresh each day, with care, consistency, and the same warmth we’d offer guests in our own home. For us, it’s not just about baking — it’s about being part of the community.
